The Attendant $$
Located in the heart of the Shoreditch Triangle, The Attendant prides itself in catering to every type of brunch customer from the on-the-go take-away customer to the hearty breakfast savorer. Visit this place to also find out about its decor that pays homage to its history as an abandoned public toilet.
